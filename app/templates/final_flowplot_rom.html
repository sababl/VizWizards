Life Expectancy Flow Map (2015)

This flow map visualizes the connections between different continents and their life expectancy rates in 2015. The lines represent the flow of data between geographical points, with line thickness indicating the strength of the relationship. Each connection has two flows: one for men and one for women, represented in different colors.
