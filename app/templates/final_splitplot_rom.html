Healthy Life Expectancy at Birth for Males (2010 vs 2021)

This visualization compares the Healthy Life Expectancy (HALE) at birth for males across different continents in 2010 and 2021 . The chart displays a grouped bar format , where each continent has two bars : a blue bar for 2010 and a red bar for 2021 . The height of each bar represents the average HALE value for that year in that continent. The data is sourced from a global dataset and filtered to include only males and HALE at birth values. This plot allows us to observe which continents have experienced an increase or decrease in life expectancy , highlighting overall trends in global health.
